MTEL - 8004

Design for Learning

Learning Objectives

  • Evaluate the impact of innovative and effective teaching and learning strategies, learning activities, technologies and assessment strategies through an in-depth understanding of the process and practice of Design for Learning
  • Evaluate strategies that create new opportunities for supporting diverse learners to engage with learning in different contexts including prior learning, facilitating learner agency, self-regulation and collaboration
  • Critically reflect on practice using Design for Learning principles to modify teaching approaches to address the needs of individuals and groups of learners
  • Coordinate learner growth using design for learning principles through regular critical feedback including assessments and support them to use this information critically to guide further learning

6.0 Presentation

Share the process you have carried out across this course in gathering student data. You will actively respond to this data by exploring, implementing and evaluating the impact of relevant design for learning strategies within your classroom. Through analysing the data you have gathered, while tracking three selected learners from one of your classes, you will demonstrate an understanding of your learners’ strengths, interests, needs, identity, language and culture. You will identify specific challenges that your learners are facing and critically reflect on your own teaching practice in relation to these areas.
