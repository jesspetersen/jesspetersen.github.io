MTEL - 8002 & 8007

Curriculum and Pedagogy and Education Leadership

Learning Objectives

  • Critically evaluate in relation to teaching practice relevant contemporary knowledge about pedagogical practice for relevant curriculum specialty learning area(s)
  • Critically reflect on the application of contemporary pedagogical approaches in curriculum area, and with peers, collaboratively devise and coordinate solutions applied in teaching practice
  • Critically evaluate in relation to practice teaching approaches, learning, assessment activities and the skills necessary in application for effective teaching, including curriculum specialist learning area(s)
  • Critically reflect on the application of contemporary pedagogical approaches in curriculum area, and with peers, collaboratively devise and coordinate solutions applied in teaching practice
  • Analyse and evaluate contemporary pedagogical theories to devise ways to adaptively apply this to effective teaching practice
  • Critically reflect on teaching approaches, learning, assessment activities in a variety of curriculum and educational contexts
  • Critique and apply through leadership initiatives that contribute to improvement of teaching practice
  • Analyse and critically reflect on practice-level leadership initiatives in specialty teaching areas
  • Critically reflect on the importance of leadership and collaboration in teaching practice for professional learning development

12.X & 13.X Report

Use excerpts from your portfolio, as well as examples of feedback from multiple sources to provide evidence of competency in your educational practice, leadership and your reflective capacity. This assessment should provide a narrative connecting your LAA and other feedback, reflection on evidence (including your own reflections on practice), with portfolio evidence including: student work, planning, PLD learning, and records of professional conversations with your learning progression as a teacher. This assessment seeks to evaluate your educational practice and your ability to engage in critical reflection.
