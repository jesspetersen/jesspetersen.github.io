About

Jess | 19 | Aspiring Software Engineer | Books, Video Games & Vanilla Cola

I am a nineteen-year-old Bachelor of Information and Communications Technology student from Auckland, New Zealand. I have a huge passion for IT and the challenges it brings. I pride myself on my ability to create innovative solutions, especially to difficult problems. I work very hard to achieve results far beyond expectations and my excellent organisational skills help me to avoid losing focus of my goals.

I am particularly interested in gaming, especially Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Games (MMORPGs) and Real-Time Strategy (RTS) games, and spend a lot of my free time absorbed in a fictional world. I aspire to, one day, be involved in the creation or maintenance of a role-playing video game.
