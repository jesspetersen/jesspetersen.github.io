About Me

I am a nineteen-year-old Bachelor of Information and Communications Technology student from Auckland, New Zealand. I have a huge passion for IT and the challenges it brings. I pride myself on my ability to create innovative solutions, especially to difficult problems. I work very hard to achieve results far beyond expectations and my excellent organisational skills help me to avoid losing focus of my goals.

I am particularly interested in gaming, especially Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Games (MMORPGs) and Real-Time Strategy (RTS) games, and spend a lot of my free time absorbed in a fictional world. I aspire to, one day, be involved in the creation or maintenance of a role-playing video game.

Abilites & Accomplishments

I have studied C# for two semesters so far, and have a good grasp of the language and how to implement it. XML was also used in my most recent semester for creating interfaces and I have spent many hours working on HTML & CSS in my own time, specifically producing and maintaining this website. I continue to experiment with other languages of interest such as Python and a small amount of both Clojure and Action Script 3.

I recently undertook an individual project to create a short and simple flash role-playing game. It required a multitude of skills and introduced several new concepts to me including agile methodologies and the use of creative commons and public domain for resources. The project represents a significant investment of my time and hard work, taking over 52 hours to complete. I am proud of what I accomplished with the project and you can play the game, or find supporting documentation here.
