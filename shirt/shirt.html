Manukau Institute of Technology

Shirt Design Competition

Welcome to the MIT Apparel Design Competition information site. Here you an find a template for your design, official rules, and find out how to submit your design.

Quick Facts

Prize: One MIT official T-Shirt featuring your design.

Closes: 11pm on Wednesday 26 October, 2016.

How to Submit: Send to studentvoice@manukau.ac.nz with your name, phone number, and design attached.

Competition Rules

  • The design is to be culturally sensitive, is not to offend others or be affiliated with any gangs.
  • The design must be appropriate for a black or white shirt/hoodie.
  • The use of the phrase "MIT" is not allowed - Manukau Institute of Technology must be written in full if it is to be used in the design.
  • If the logo is used it may not be altered and must be at least 65 mm in height, it must also be used in its entirety.
  • Your design must be wholly original. By submitting a design you are guaranteeing that you hold rights to everything in it and that it does not contain any copyright material.
  • Entries may only be submitted by current students.
  • Students may make more than one entry.
  • All entries must be received by 11pm on 26 October 2016.
  • All entry emails should include your name and phone number.
  • Entries should be sent to studentvoice@manukau.ac.nz with the subject line "T-Shirt competition".
  • The winning design will be chosen by vote by the Student Council.
  • The winner will be notified by email and phone by 2 November 2016.
  • The winner will receive one free shirt or hoodie once apparel has begun distribution.
  • Submission of an entry grants Manukau Institute of Technology and its agents an unlimited, worldwide, perpetual, royalty-free license and right to publish, use, edit, adapt, modify and/or publicly perform the entry in any way, in commerce and in any and all media.
