    • Digital Technologies focus on understanding, resolving and creating.

    The Digital Technology area is a department based to strengthen the positioning of digital technologies in the New Zealand Curriculum and Te Marautanga o Aotearoa. This area is available to students from years 1-13. In Papatoetoe High School, we are able to choose a subject related to digital at year 9. This enables students to express problems and create solutions in methods that means a computer can be used to solve. Students learn primary programming concepts and how to take advantage of the capabilites of a computer.

    Here are the subjects which are available within the Digital Technologies section:

  • - Digital Web [DTW]
  • - Digital Programming [DTP]
  • - Digital Media [DTM]
  • - Digital Networks [DTN]
  • - Digital Certificate [DTC]
  • - Digital Tech [DTG]
