Introduction to Digital Technology

Digital Technology is a foundation course for computer programming and web/digital communication courses.

It is designed to help you better understand, communicate and adapt to a digital world as it impacts our personal lifes, society and the business world.
It also allows you to learn and enhance your skills through digital programming, web design and media with hands-on activities and assessment focused tasks. Allowing Students to not only understand the concepts, but apply their knowledge to given situations and tasks.
