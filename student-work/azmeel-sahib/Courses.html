Available Courses

Digital Media(DTM)

This course is aimed at students who want to gain skills in industry standard IT software. The course is designed around the New Zealand Certificate in Computing and is a two-year course. Over the two-year course students from year 11, 12 or 13 have access to 40 credits in a range of different software programs, such as word, excel, access, power-point and more.

Digital Programming(DTP)

This course has been designed to allow students to explore the different aspects of digital media through technological practice. They are given the opportunity to design and create their own technological outcomes through the use of computer languages whilst learning and practising digital technology skills. This course is a basis for DTM2 & DTM3.

Digital Web Design(DTW)

This course has been designed for students who have an interest in web design and have a suitable level of confidence on computers. It is a course with a large amount of practical element but does involve both unit and achievement standards.
